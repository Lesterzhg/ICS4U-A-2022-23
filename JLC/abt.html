About Us

Our Service and Dedicated Teachers

Welcome to Jenny Li Learning Center. It is primarily for children program aged from 4 up to 18, who are keen to learn creative kids art, cartooning, storyteller and storyboard; Fine arts have sketches, paintings, sculpture, computer graphic design; portfolio class for university and art high school.

Jenny Li

Art Teacher

Li established Jenny Learning Centre ( JLC ) in the early 2000’s, following graduating from Art School in Wuhan, China, obtaining a Bachelors of Fine Art Degree from Hubei Fine Art University in 1990, and graduating with honours from Sheridan College’s Post Graduate Multimedia Program and Sheridan’s College Post Graduate Animation Program.

Jenny followed her passion to pursue art instruction through JLC for students who aspired to developing their own artistic capabilities and desires. Jenny and her students have had great success. Jenny has won many teaching awards and accolades. Her students have gone on to the top art high schools in the GTA, to the Ontario College of Art and Design and art and architecture programs in many Universities. To do so, Jenny worked with her students to prepare and submit meritorious art portfolios reflecting their own passion and accomplishments.

Hazel Nonan

Art Tutor

Hazel is a Visual Art student of York University won York University Scholarship. She is very good at communicating with students, patients as well. She loves to share her contemporary art experience with students.
